Katie Ann

Professional Advisor, CICA, DV Survivors

I am a dedicated and compassionate professional counselor, committed to positively impacting the lives of individuals who have experienced domestic violence. With a BA Hons degree in Social Work and extensive experience in frontline children and family practice from 2021, I bring a deep understanding and wealth of knowledge to my practice. My passion lies in empowering people to reclaim, find hope, and heal from the trauma they have endured.
Having worked closely with many survivors of abuse, I am well aware of the impact that domestic violence can have on a person’s physical, emotional and mental well-being My goal is to provide support, guidance and resources a to guide individuals through the arduous process of recovery. I believe in creating a safe and supportive environment where survivors can regain their confidence and rebuild their lives.

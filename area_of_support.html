How We Can Support You

If you are suffering from any of these problems ,we can contact you

Emotional Harm

Our support team is here to help you heal from the psychological trauma caused by emotional abuse, ensuring you regain your confidence and sense of self-worth.

Physical Harm

We provide resources and support to help you recover from physical harm and feel safe again.

Sexual Harm

We offer a safe space and professional guidance to help you navigate through the healing process and reclaim your life after experiencing sexual harm.

Harm in Social Care Settings

We are dedicated to supporting victims of abuse in social care settings, such as residential care homes, ensuring they receive the care and justice they deserve.

Institutional Abuse

We provide specialized support to help survivors of sexual abuse within institutions like prisons, care homes, or foster care find their voice and achieve justice.

Educational Institution Abuse

We support students and staff affected by sexual harm in schools, colleges, or universities in finding safety and pursuing justice.

Family Home Abuse

Our support services are designed to help victims of family abuse find safety, support, and a path to recovery.

Workplace Abuse

We offer resources and support to help you address and overcome workplace abuse, impacting your professional and personal life.

Child and Adult Sexual Abuse

We provide comprehensive support for both child and adult survivors of sexual abuse, ensuring they receive the care and justice they need.

Police Support: Claire's Law and Sarah’s Law

Our team can guide you through the processes of Claire's Law and Sarah’s Law to help you protect yourself and your loved ones.

If you have been affected by any of these issues, please contact us for confidential support and guidance.

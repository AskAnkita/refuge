About Me

I have been a qualified social worker with a BA Hons degree since 2021. My experience has been in front-line children and family services. I am dedicated to helping individuals thrive after experiencing various forms of abuse. My passion lies in empowering people to reclaim their lives and find hope while healing.

I have stepped away from front line services but I am still extremely passionate about helping victims of domestic violence gain their voice, life and sparkle back therefore I have started my own campaign on empowering women, men and other identified genders in reclaiming their life’s.

My qualifications:

  • CFA accredited
  • BA Hons soical work degree
  • Humanities and social sciences diploma
  • Motivational interviewing trained
  • CSE awareness
  • County lines trained
  • DV awareness course completed
  • Child to parent : parent to child harm trained.

my goal is to undertake further training to be able to offer more knowledgeable insight

How I will empower and support you:
With my expertise in enabling and helping individuals take back their power from experiencing traumatic events in their lives, I aim to help you rebuild your confidence during these early stages of taking back full control of your life. In addition to my support I was also be able to refer to you some great supporting charities who will support you throughout the next steps of your recovery.

Next steps:
After a short conversation with me to ensure all the criteria is met; I will put you in contact with a solicitors who then will support you through the process of gaining compensation for the events you have encountered which are not your fault. My promise to you is; I will ensure I remain supportive to you throughout this whole process. Let me help you take back control of your life gaining that self sparkle back.
